About PyDial

What is PyDial?

PyDial is your AI-powered phone assistant that makes calls on your behalf. Simply describe your task, provide the contact details, and let PyDial handle the rest.

How It Works

  1. Purchase minutes for your account
  2. Describe your task (e.g., schedule an appointment, confirm a reservation)
  3. Provide the contact's phone number
  4. PyDial makes the call using natural language AI
  5. Receive a detailed email summary of the call and its outcomes

Key Features

  • Natural conversational AI that sounds human
  • Pay-per-minute pricing - only pay for what you use
  • Detailed call summaries via email
  • Handle multiple types of tasks:
    • Appointment scheduling
    • Reservation management
    • Information gathering
    • Follow-up calls
    • And more...
  • Available 24/7 - make calls at any time
